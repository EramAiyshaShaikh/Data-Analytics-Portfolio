## 📌 Project Overview

This project uses SQL to analyze flight and airport data and uncover insights related to passenger traffic, flight frequency, route performance, airport activity, seat utilization, destination popularity, and the relationship between city population and air traffic.

The analysis uses SQL queries, Common Table Expressions (CTEs), aggregations, joins, and calculated metrics to answer key business questions related to the aviation industry.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The main objectives of this project are to:

- Analyze total passenger traffic across routes and time.
- Calculate average passengers per flight for different routes and airports.
- Identify high-traffic flight corridors.
- Compare passenger numbers across origin cities and airports.
- Evaluate seat utilization across airlines.
- Identify popular destination airports based on inbound passenger traffic.
- Analyze the relationship between city population and airport passenger traffic.
- Examine the impact of population size on flight frequency and route choices.

## 🔍 Key Analysis

### 1. Passenger Traffic by Route and Time

Analyzed total passenger traffic by year, quarter, origin city, and destination city to understand passenger movement over time.

---

### 2. Average Passengers per Flight

Calculated average passenger counts across different routes and airlines.

The analysis also evaluates average passenger traffic associated with individual airports.

---

### 3. High-Traffic Flight Corridors

Analyzed flight frequency between origin and destination cities to identify routes with the highest number of flights.

---

### 4. Top-Performing Origin Airports

Compared passenger volumes across origin cities and airports to identify airports with higher passenger activity.

---

### 5. Seat Utilization Analysis

Estimated average seat utilization for airlines by comparing passenger counts with the maximum observed passenger capacity.

---

### 6. Popular Destination Airports

Analyzed inbound passenger traffic to identify destination airports receiving higher passenger volumes.

---

### 7. Population vs Passenger Traffic

Combined airport passenger data with city population data to examine the relationship between population size and airport passenger traffic.

---

### 8. Population vs Flight Frequency

Compared total flight frequency between routes with the combined population of their origin and destination cities.

This analysis helps explore whether population size is associated with route frequency.

---

## 💡 Key Insights

The SQL analysis focuses on identifying:

- High-volume passenger routes.
- Airports with higher passenger activity.
- High-frequency flight corridors.
- Popular destination airports.
- Differences in airline seat utilization.
- The relationship between city population and passenger traffic.
- The relationship between population size and flight frequency.
