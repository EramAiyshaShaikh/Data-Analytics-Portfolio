## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Sales Performance Dashboard** developed using Microsoft Power BI.

The dashboard analyzes sales performance across products, time periods, promotions, cities, and key business metrics. It provides interactive filtering capabilities that allow users to explore sales trends and compare product performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The main objectives of this project are to:

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Identify top-performing products by sales.
- Identify products with the lowest sales performance.
- Compare products by quantity sold.
- Analyze product profitability.
- Track sales trends over time.
- Compare Net Sales and Total Sales.
- Analyze profit performance.
- Evaluate promotion performance.
- Analyze sales performance across cities.
- Use interactive filters to explore the data.

## 📊 Dashboard Analysis

### 🏆 Top 5 Products by Sales

The dashboard identifies the top-performing products based on total sales.

The top products include:

- Apple iPhone 14
- Apple MacBook Air
- Sony Bravia 55" TV
- Samsung Galaxy S21
- HP Pavilion Laptop

---

### 📉 Bottom 5 Products by Sales

The dashboard also highlights products with the lowest sales performance, including:

- Tupperware Lunch Box
- L'Oreal Shampoo
- Nivea Body Lotion
- Dove Soap Pack
- Colgate Toothpaste

---

### 📦 Product Quantity Analysis

The dashboard compares products based on quantity sold and identifies the top and bottom performers.

---

### 💰 Profit Analysis

The dashboard provides a comparison of product profitability and identifies the highest- and lowest-profit products.

---

### 📈 Sales Trend Over Time

The dashboard tracks sales performance over time, allowing users to analyze changes in sales across the available date range.

---

### 🎟️ Promotion Analysis

The dashboard compares average discount values across different promotional campaigns, including:

- Weekend Flash Sale
- Clearance Sale
- Summer Sale
- New Year Special
- Festive Diwali

---

### 🌍 City-Level Sales Analysis

The dashboard provides a geographical view of sales performance across cities, helping identify differences in sales activity across locations.

---

## 🔎 Interactive Filters

The dashboard includes interactive filters that allow users to analyze the data based on:

- Date
- Customer Name
- Product Name
- Promotion Name

These filters make it easier to explore specific segments of the sales data.

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Apple iPhone 14 was the leading product by sales and profit in the dashboard analysis.
- Apple MacBook Air and Sony Bravia 55" TV also ranked among the strongest products by sales.
- Tupperware Lunch Box and Colgate Toothpaste were among the lowest-performing products by sales and profit.
- The dashboard enables analysis of sales trends across multiple years.
- Promotion-level analysis provides insight into discount values associated with different campaigns.
- City-level analysis helps compare sales activity across different locations.
